CAJUN CATFISH HONEY DIJON WITH BACON POTATO SALAD

Description

Indulge in the harmonious medley of flavors with our delectable Cajun Catfish Honey Dijon paired with a sumptuous Bacon Potato Salad. Immerse yourself in the bold Cajun seasoning that cloaks the tender catfish fillet, offering a symphony of smoky, spicy, and subtly sweet notes that pay homage to the Southern bayous. As the catfish is grilled to perfection, it becomes a canvas for the velvety allure of honey Dijon glaze that adds a touch of sophistication to this rustic dish. Complementing this exquisite main course is our Bacon Potato Salad, where velvety potatoes harmonize with the salty embrace of crispy bacon, creating a symphony of textures. The salad dances with freshness from vibrant herbs, a burst of tang from Dijon mustard, and a whisper of sweetness from honey. Together, this culinary masterpiece transports you to the heart of Louisiana's rich culinary heritage, promising a palate-enchanting experience that captures the essence of Southern comfort with every tantalizing bite.

Cajun Catfish Ingredients

  • 4 U.S. Farm-Raised Catfish Fillets
  • ½ cup fish breading
  • 1 tablespoon Creole seasoning
  • 1½ to 2 cups frying oil

Honey Dijon Bacon Potato Salad Ingredients

  • 5 cups baby Yukon gold potatoes, cooked and quartered
  • 1 medium red onion, diced
  • 1½ cups celery, diced
  • 1 cup bacon, cooked, drained and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Creole mustard
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hot sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Combine fish breading and Creole seasoning in shallow bowl. Coat catfish in breading, lightly shaking off any excess.
  2. In large skillet, heat oil over high heat. Fry catfish until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side. Place on paper towel to drain.
  3. Serve immediately with Honey Dijon Bacon Potato Salad.

    4. Honey Dijon Bacon Potato Salad

  4. Combine all ingredients and mix well. Adjust seasoning with salt, pepper and hot sauce. Cover and refrigerate.
